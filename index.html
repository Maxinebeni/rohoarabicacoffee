Our Coffee

...... Roho Coffee is grown in the Kayonza District, Rwanda’s Eastern province, in partnership with the Akagera Coffee Project. Roho Coffee is pure Arabica bourbon coffee. It is grown at an altitude of 1750 meters above sea level. Roho Coffee’s goal is to source exotic and vivid specialty coffee, that s as colorful and as the people of Rwanda, while supporting women coffee farmers by implementing fair trade policies across its value chain.
